For Associations

Easy

Founding a new association can be a pain. Opening a bank account for your association requires some paper works. Our service provides an out of the box experience - using crypto-currencies.

Fast

Add new members, decide on issues, vote on board members. All tasks can be conducted within seconds.

Secure

All data is stored on the Ethereum blockchain. It is secure and immutable. No one can edit what has been decided upon. A transparent record of membership and transaction history builds trust.
